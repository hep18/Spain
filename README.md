## Bottom line up front

| Budget | Verdict |
|---|---|
| **$5,000** | **Not feasible as requested.** Round-trip flights for 2 adults alone consume $4,600–$5,200. To fit $5K, you must drop Paris OR skip the Pamplona balcony OR have the friend cover all his own costs AND accept hostel-tier lodging. See "$5K Cut-Down Version" in the itinerary. |
| **$7,000** | **Tight but workable** if the friend pays his own share of San Sebastián lodging and the Pamplona day. Mid-tier flights, central but modest lodging, group Pamplona balcony + bus, group wine tour. Total estimated **$6,800–$7,400** for 2 paying adults. |
| **$8,500–$9,500** (recommended) | Comfortably covers nonstop AA flights, central lodging including the splurge-worthy Hotel Niza on La Concha, private transfers, premium balcony location, and a private Rioja wine tour with door-to-door dropoff in San Sebastián. |
